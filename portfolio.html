Amazing Space Page

The Amazing Space Page is a collaborative project created to provide relevant, synthesized information regarding current happenings in space. Features of the page include a map of the International Space Station's position in orbit (that refreshes in real time), a real-time count of astronauts currently in space, and information and pictures regarding upcoming and recent SpaceX launches.
